Why Join BritishVets.co.uk?

Connecting locums directly with veterinary practices – no middlemen, no agency fees.

What is BritishVets.co.uk?

BritishVets.co.uk has over **1,000 veterinary practice addresses**. This is your opportunity to **get visibility and connect** with them!

All locums who sign up before launch will get a **free listing for 2025**.

Your Skills, Your Terms

For years, recruitment companies have taken a massive cut just for connecting locums with practices.

Many locums don’t need recruiters—or perhaps **recruiters need to step up their game**!

That’s why BritishVets.co.uk is **a locum directory** where **vets and nurses** can list themselves, making it easier for practices to **find and contact them directly**.

How Does It Work?

  • ✔️ **Sign up for free** (before launch, all listings are free for 2025).
  • ✔️ Practices browse locum profiles.
  • ✔️ They contact you directly – no agencies, no commissions.
