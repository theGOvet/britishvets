BritishVets.co.uk: A New Way for Locums to Connect with Practices

For years, veterinary locums have relied on recruitment agencies that take a significant cut just for connecting them with practices. BritishVets.co.uk is changing that.

We’re building a locum-only directory where veterinary surgeons and nurses can list themselves, making it easier for practices to find and contact them directly—no middlemen, no commissions. Unlike traditional job boards, this platform puts locums in control, allowing them to set their own terms and be contacted by practices that meet their criteria.

Withhundreds of veterinary practice already mapped, BritishVets.co.uk is actively bringing the practices to the locums—not the other way around. Our goal is to shift the power balance, making recruitment simpler, fairer, and more transparent.

How It Works:

Locums sign up and create a profile (free for early registrants).

Practices browse and contact locums directly.

No agencies, no hidden fees, just direct connections.

BritishVets.co.uk is currently gathering momentum, and we’re inviting locums and practices to be part of this movement. If you're a locum and have suggestions on how the directory should look, don't hesitate to let us know upon registration.
